About the Project

This plaque is part of a nationwide accessibility project initated by a company specializing in facilities for people with disabilites.


It serves as a certification plaque provided by the city to businesses-such as restaurants and cafes in Seongnam-that have installed wheelchair ramps
to improve accessibility. This design was distributed to certified establishments in Seongnam and later adapted for a similar project in Suwon.
